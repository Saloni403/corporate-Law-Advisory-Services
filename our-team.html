He has thorough knowledge on the various corporate laws viz, Companies Act, SEBI Regulations, Foreign Exchange Laws, IPR Laws, etc. He heads the Secretarial Audit & related services, Due Diligence, ESOP design and implementation and Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) practice divisions at Corporate Liasoning Experts (CLE)
