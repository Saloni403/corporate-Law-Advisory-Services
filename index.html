Profile

Welcome To Corporate Liasoning Experts (CLE).

Corporate Liasoning Experts is a leading corporate consulting firm dedicated to help professionals (CA’s, CS’s, Advocates & CMA’s) on PAN India basis by navigating their complex challenges and by providing appropriate and desired solutions in expediate manner. Our firm focuses on the difficulties and issues that professionals face on a day to day basis. Our vision is to become a constant support for the professionals who face challenges in dealing with departments and regulatory authorities (like CRC, CPC, CPACE and others) due to various reasons.

Our Team works seamlessly across practice areas and offices and are very well exposed in dealing with various regulatory authorities like Registrar of Companies (ROC), Regional Director (RD), National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Securities & Exchange Board of India (SEBI), Stock Exchange(s), Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Registrar of Trademark & Copyrights etc.
