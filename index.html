Profile

Welcome To Corporate Liasoning Experts (CLE).

Corporate Liasoning Experts (CLE) is a firm of Company Secretaries having practical experience in diverse field of services. The firm has offices in New Delhi, Noida, Mumbai, Lucknow and Kolkata. We also operate with local teams of associates across the country. We aspire for professional growth and development, both as individuals and as a business. Our core values of excellence, commitment, diligence, governance and responsibility continues to fuel this growth. The services of SRC are utilized by several entrepreneurs and Chartered firms also for various assignments.

Our team works seamlessly across practice areas and offices and are very well exposed in dealing with various regulatory authorities like Registrar of Companies (ROC), Regional Director (RD), National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT), Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA), Securities & Exchange Board of India (SEBI), Stock Exchange(s), Reserve Bank of India (RBI), Registrar of Trademark & Copyrights etc.
